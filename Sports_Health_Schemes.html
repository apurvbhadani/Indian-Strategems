Health & Sports Schemes

1. Free Education For Sports Medal Winners / Participants Of National/ International Events


The scheme's goal is to give financial aid to medal winners and outstanding athletes studying at universities/colleges leading to all full-time regular programmes, including PG Diplomas and other professional courses.

2. Nirman Shramik Antrashtriya Khel Pratiyogiyo Hetu Protsahan Yojana


The goal of this programme is to encourage registered beneficiaries who work in construction and other related fields, as well as their children, to engage in international sports.


Click here to Apply

3. Dhyan Chand Award For Life-time Achievement Sports And Games


To recognise athletes who have contributed to sports by their performance and who continue to contribute to sports promotion even after they have retired from active athletic careers.


Click here to Apply

4. Cash Awards To Medal Winners In International Sports Events And Their Coaches


To reward excellent athletes' achievements, to inspire and motivate them to attain even greater heights, and to serve as inspiring role models for recruiting the next generation to participate in sports.


Click here to Apply

5. Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay National Welfare Fund For Sportspersons


The initiative supports the general wellbeing of athletes in order to relieve suffering among them and their dependents. The system can be offered to active athletes individually or as a group.


Click here to Apply

6. Scheme For Pension And Medical Aid To Artistes


A MoC pension programme aimed at improving the financial and socioeconomic circumstances of elderly artists and academics who have made major contributions in their respective disciplines but are forced to live in poverty owing to their age.


Click here to Apply

7. Mukhyamantri Udiyaman Khiladi Unnayan Yojana


The major goal of the Mukhyamantri Udiyaman Khiladi Unnayan Yojana is to inspire all aspiring athletes in the state of Uttarakhand. This would encourage athletes who are excelling in sports to stay involved and participate in sports with more zeal.


Click here to Apply

8. Scheme For Arjuna Awards For Outstanding Performance In Sports And Games


The Arjuna Award is India's highest athletic honour, presented by the Government of India (Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports) on athletes for great achievement in international and national competitions, as well as leadership characteristics and moral character.


Click here to Apply

9. Scheme For Dronacharya Award For Outstanding Coaches In Sports And Games


This award is intended to honour coaches who have consistently done outstanding and meritorious work that has enabled athletes to excel in international events and to motivate coaches to devote themselves solely to raising the standard of athletes to peak performance.


Click here to Apply

10 Scheme For Adolescent Girls


A support scheme by Ministry of Women & Child Development enabling self-development and empowerment of out-of-school adolescent girls, and improving their nutritional, health & hygiene status.


Click here to Apply

11. Beti Bachao Beti Padhao


In 2015, the Indian government introduced the Beti Bachao, Beti Padhao (BBBP) scheme to address concerns about gender discrimination and women empowerment in the country. The name Beti Bachao, Beti Padhao translates to ‘Save the girl child, educate the girl child’.


Click here to Apply

12. Ayushman Bharat - Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana


Ayushman Bharat, a flagship scheme of the Government of India, was launched as recommended by the National Health Policy 2017, to achieve the vision of Universal Health Coverage (UHC).
